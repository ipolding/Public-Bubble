The Public Bubble

The Public Bubble is run by and for graduates on public sector graduate schemes, including the Local Government Association’s NGDP, the Civil Service Fast Stream, Teach First, Police Now, Frontline and the NHS Graduate Scheme.

We offer a thinking space for public sector graduates, fostering collaboration at the grassroots of the sector. Our events are all free and include professional training events, speaking events, policy cafes and socials. Check out our events page for more info on what we’ve done, and what you can get involved in next.

The Team

The Public Bubble is run by a committee of volunteers, and we’re all public sector graduates from across a range of schemes.
